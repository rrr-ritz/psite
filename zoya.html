  • Wednesday, June 21

    Summary:

    1. Visual intuition for fractions. Discussed the actual meaning of fractions and how they can be represented with a picture/diagram. Lots of practice and method provided for how to draw these pictures, and how they can be useful in understanding a fractional number.

    2. Improper vs mixed fractions. What does it mean to have 9/8 pizzas? One interpretation is that you have 9 slices of pizza; another is that you have one whole pizza and an extra slice (1 1/8). When is each interpretation useful and how can we convert from one to another?

    3. Intro to multiplying and dividing fractions + links between fractions and division. Introduced the concept of multiplying and dividing fractions and their applications. Showed how just like multiplication is just repeated addition, and division is just repeated subtraction, multiplication and division are both two sides of the same coin (i.e., multiplying by 1/2 and dividing by 2 are the same). Showed how to perform the actual multiplication and division of fractions, and practiced it.
